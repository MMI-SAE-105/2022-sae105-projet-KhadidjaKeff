The view’s worth the detour

Walk, see you on the way up !

To recharge your mental or exercise your body, San Juan de Gaztelugatxe will welcome you with its view that’s worth the detour.

Here, we are on the Basque coast, San Juan de Gaztelugatxe, its name is complicated but the view’s worth the detour. To go to San Juan de Gaztelugatxe you’ll need a whole morning, 4h30 at the most, don't forget the sandwiches and maybe more clothes ! You will have to be mentally prepared to accomplish this small feat, but every step forward, no matter how small, is already an achievement.

The “Grande Traversée du Jura” as its name indicates it’s a large hiking trail of 400 km, it will take more than 20 days to complete correctly. It’s better to go there with friends or family so to not feel alone for long, but whether alone or accompanied, you will succeed your goals.

The GR 58 is a very popular and well-loved hiking trail. This trail is well occupied and therefore very accessible, its alpine paths are very beautiful and captivate anyone who takes them. Don’t forget to hydrate while admiring. During this 130 km journey, you can also meet a lot of animals and people who enjoys the view. So take something to draw them!
