Travel differently, seize the moment.

Diary Intimate, Zep

Carnet Intime is not just a collection of illustrations and intimate notes, it is the way that Zep has to find to capture the moment, to live it differently, by drawing, by telling it. Today, to capture the moment and remember it, we tend to rely on our phones and photograph, yet according to a 2013 study by Linda A. Henkel in the scientific journal Psychological Science, The brain is more likely to forget what we took. The brain tends to walk like a folder tree, if there is a digital photo that serves as a memory, why create a copy in the brain?

Neither an artist Nor a poet

During his travels in Japan, Tanzania and France, Zep drew to capture the beauty of the world, to better remember and explore it.

“The world doesn’t get better because I draw it. I draw it to understand that it is beautiful. “

Zep, by his real name Philippe Chappuis is a comic book author, colorist and swiss illustrator. Zep was born in Onex in 1967 and he later went to the “Centre de Formation Artistique” in Geneva. In 1992, he created the eponymous character of Titeuf known worldwide. In 2004, he received the Grand Prize of the city of Angoulême for all his works.
